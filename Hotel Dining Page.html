Maison Rostang

A restaurant passed down through generations in the Rostang family, Maison Rostang continues through the great-grandson Michel Rostang. Here, the chefs will welcome you to Paris for a fine dining experience in a beautiful setting. You cannot walk-in with this restaurant, you must make a reservation through their website.

Address: 20 Rue Rennequin, Paris, 75017, France
