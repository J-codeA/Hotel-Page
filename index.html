About us
From 1990 to today, this is how it all started. From humble beginnings to the great powerhouse we are known for now.

1990
Founder Juwan Alexander Aguila started with a modest hotel in Paris, France. Only five floors with barely enough staff to cover all the positions needed at the bare minimum.

1995
Within five years, Aguila towers doubled in staff and resources, allowing the hotel to expand up to thirty floors and hire enough staff to accomodate for all the guest staying

2000
We gained a partnership with Bears and Raccoons, becoming one of our main dining places our guests would go to!

2001
Recongnition expanded when French celebrity Emmanuelle Seigner, most known for her roles at The Diving Bell and the Butterfly (2007), The Ninth Gate (1999) and Frantic (1988). Staying with us, she geniunely enjoyed her stay with us and advertised us with no charge!

2007
We partnered up with SEFA Events as one of the main hotel locations for guests to stay as they come for Comic-Con Paris (formerly known as Kultima). Allowing a huge influx of guests and celebrity guests which allow us to expand out business even farther. From then on, we remained partners with the event even until today!

2010
With the rise of WiFi, we pride ourselves with making sure that our hotel is up to date with the evolving world around us. We began implementing Wifi in every room of the hotel allowing our guest to be connected to the internet at no additional cost!

2013
With our growing successful, our founder decided to expand out of country! We opened our first international hotel in London, England on May 12, 2013!

2015
Our success allowed us to get a partnerrship with the fine dining services of Maison Rostang! Now our guests can reserve a seat in one of the best dining restaraunts in France!

2019
Still going strong, our founder is currently trying to find other countries and cities to expand to! We appreciate all those who stayed with us, gave us feedback, critiques, and helped us make the necessary changes to make this hotel brand the powerhouse it is today! We thank all of you for this!
